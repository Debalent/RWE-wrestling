## ğŸ‘Š Managing Wrestlers

### Adding a New Wrestler

1. Click **Wrestler** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **+** button (top right)
3. Fill in the information:

   **Required Fields:**
   - **Wrestler Name**: Full ring name (e.g., "The Undertaker")
   - **Slug**: Click "Generate" - this creates the web address
   
   **Optional Fields:**
   - **Nickname**: Catchphrase or alternate name
   - **Profile Image**: Click to upload a photo
   - **Biography**: Tell their story
   - **Height**: e.g., "6'2""
   - **Weight**: e.g., "220 lbs"
   - **Hometown**: Where they're from
   - **Finishing Move**: Their signature move
   - **Championships Won**: Click "Add item" for each title
   - **Social Media Links**: Add Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok
   - **Status**: Active, Injured, or Inactive
   - **Featured Wrestler**: Toggle ON to show on homepage

4. Click **Publish** (bottom right)

### Editing a Wrestler

1. Click **Wrestler** in sidebar
2. Find and click the wrestler's name
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Publish** to save

### Deleting a Wrestler

1. Open the wrestler's profile
2. Click the **...** menu (top right)
3. Select **Delete**
4. Confirm deletion

## ğŸ—“ï¸ Managing Events

### Creating a New Event

1. Click **Event** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **+** button
3. Fill in the details:

   **Event Information:**
   - **Event Title**: Name of the show (e.g., "Summer Slam 2024")
   - **Slug**: Click "Generate"
   - **Event Date & Time**: Click the calendar icon
   - **Venue Name**: Where it's happening
   - **Venue Address**: Full street address
   - **City** and **State**
   
   **Visual & Details:**
   - **Event Poster**: Upload the event poster image
   - **Event Description**: What fans can expect
   - **Ticket Purchase Link**: URL where fans buy tickets
   - **Ticket Price**: e.g., "$15 - $25"
   
   **Match Card:**
   - Click **Add item** under "Match Card"
   - For each match, add:
     - Match Type (Singles, Tag Team, Championship, etc.)
     - Participants (e.g., "John Doe vs Jane Smith")
     - Match Description (optional storyline details)
   
   **Settings:**
   - **Event Status**: Upcoming, Live Now, Completed, or Cancelled
   - **Featured Event**: Toggle ON to highlight on homepage

4. Click **Publish**

### Updating an Event

Perfect for adding matches or changing details:

1. Click **Event** in sidebar
2. Find your event
3. Make changes
4. Click **Publish**

**Pro Tip**: Change status to "Live Now" during the event, then "Completed" after!

3. Fill in product details:

## ğŸ“° Managing News & Announcements

3. Fill in:

   - **Title**: Headline

     - Insert images
     - Create quotes

   - **Published At**: When to publish (defaults to now)

   - **Featured Post**: Show prominently on homepage
